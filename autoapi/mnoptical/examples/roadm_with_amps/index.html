mnoptical.examples.roadm_with_amps¶

This script shows how to create Terminals by passing Transceiver objects to their constructor method (lines 64-67); it also shows how to create ROADMs by passing boost and preamp Amplifier objects to their constructor method (lines 72-74)

Simple linear topology: h1 - s1 - t1 – r1 – t2 - s2 - h2

Transmitting 1 channel from t1 to t2
